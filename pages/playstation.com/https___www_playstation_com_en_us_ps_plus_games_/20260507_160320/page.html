Stacking or changing your plan

Frequently asked questions

  • PlayStation Plus Essential members can play the selection of monthly games that are updated at the beginning of every month as well as the entire PlayStation Plus Collection library.
  • PlayStation Plus Extra members can enjoy all game content from PlayStation Plus Essential, as well as download access to the Game Catalog, which features hundreds of games.
  • PlayStation Plus Premium members can play all of the games included in the PlayStation Plus Essential and Extra plans, and choose to download or stream anything from the Games Catalog and the Classics Catalog. The PlayStation Plus Premium membership plan also unlocks limited-time access to select top games in the Game Trials library.

At any time, you can purchase a new membership that will start when your existing plan ends.

To change from your current plan to a lower tier plan, sign-in to the settings page for your account for PlayStation and choose the Subscriptions tab to manage your PlayStation Plus membership plan. Your membership will continue on a lower tier plan after the next payment date.

To move to a higher tier membership plan, you can upgrade immediately by purchasing your chosen plan. You will pay a pro-rated fee to bring your payments to the level of your new plan for the remainder of the paid membership period.

You can add the monthly games to your game library to download and play at any time as long as you have an active membership plan. The Game Catalog and Classics Catalog will also be available to members of their corresponding membership plan, but some games may have an end date when they will be removed from the PlayStation Plus service.

Most games available across the different PlayStation Plus game libraries are available to download to your console*. The Classics Catalog also features a wide selection of classic PlayStation 3 games that are available via streaming only.  PlayStation Plus Premium members can also choose to stream games from the Game Catalog and Classics Catalog to their PS4 or PS5 console or to a PC.

*PS5 games can only be downloaded to a PlayStation 5 console.

Cloud streaming is a PlayStation Plus Premium benefit available on PS4 and PS5 consoles as well as PC. Select the streaming option from a game listing and you should be able to play after a short wait. You can view PC streaming requirements here.

The cloud streaming benefit allows streaming of select games to your PlayStation 5 console or PlayStation Portal remote player. You can stream a wide range of PS5 games downloadable from PlayStation Store, hundreds of titles in the Game Catalogue and Classics Catalogue, and select free-to-play games. Use the Cloud Streaming Game Finder to find games eligible to stream.
